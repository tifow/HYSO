Horsed Youth Sports Organization It is a non-governmental organization and non-profite organization It was founded in 2023 in Mugadishu Somaliya and works to help young people get a basic education and play football with the help of local organizations and international organizations based on the need Horsed Youth Sports Organization it started the hard work that the society needs

OUR MISSION

-Our Mission is to help youn people who love playing football and athletics such as t-shirts, shoes, accessories, finance and capturing the trophy non-profit Organization
-help clubs to get sports equipment such as sports shoes, jerseys and balls to continue playing and to produce young professionals and progress in sports.
-participation in the construction of football stadiums and their security.
-helping clubs to travel to play against other clubs or to participate in leagues in other areas for social interaction

,The Horsed Youth Sports Organization, recognizing the benefits that football brings to the community, has worked hard to support sports clubs as much as possible. Their aim is to help create a balanced society where everyone has the opportunity to play, and to nurture young athletes with the skills they need. The organization has assisted sports clubs by providing football kits, financial support for purchasing sports shoes, and covering the travel expenses needed during away games. This support ensures that young players can continue to grow and excel in their sport without facing financial difficulties.

Education

Education is the key to progress and life transformation, especially for the underprivileged.
It is a powerful tool that helps people access new opportunities that improve their lives. Here, we will highlight the benefits of education for the poor:
1-Increased Job Opportunities:Education equips individuals with new skills and knowledge, enabling them to access better-paying jobs.
2-Improved Knowledge:Education enhances understanding and awareness, allowing individuals to participate more effectively in society.
3-Economic Growth:Education helps the underprivileged understand economics and financial management, enabling them to handle their finances better.
4-mproved Health:Health education helps individuals understand disease prevention and adopt healthier lifestyles.
5-Leadership Development:Education prepares individuals to become leaders who can guide communities and create positive change.
6-Cultural Enrichment:Education helps individuals preserve and promote their cultural heritage, while also fostering respect for diverse cultures.
7-Increased Business Opportunities:Educated individuals from poor backgrounds are more likely to access successful business opportunities.
8-Attracting New Opportunities:Education opens doors to new career, academic, and social opportunities.
9-Innovation and New Ideas:Education fosters creativity, generating new ideas that can lead to positive societal changes.
10-Increased Accountability and Responsibility:Education instills a sense of responsibility and encourages individuals to contribute positively to their communities.
11-Work-Life Balance:Education helps individuals balance work, family, and personal life more effectively.
12-Personal Growth*: Education helps individuals develop themselves by providing new knowledge and skills, improving their overall quality of life.
In conclusion:education is the key to success for the underprivileged, enhancing their opportunities, economy, and future prospects.
